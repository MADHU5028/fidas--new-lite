Introducing FIDAS Lite – The Smart Fabric Inspection Software

In the competitive textile industry, manual defect logging is becoming a bottleneck for growth. To stay ahead, mills are turning to Manual fabric inspection systems to ensure precision and speed. While high-end automation was once a major capital expense.Change the game with FIDAS Lite.

Designed specifically for small-size textile mills, mid-size textile mills and new adopters of Industry, FIDAS Lite offers a professional fabric inspection platform that is both affordable and easy to deploy.

Why FIDAS Lite is the Best Choice for Mid-Size Mills

FIDAS Lite moves away from expensive, stationary PC setups in favor of an Industrial HMI Node ecosystem . This makes it one of the most flexible textile quality control solutions on the market today.

  • Cross-Platform Accessibility: The application is accessible via any web browser. You can monitor your floor from a PC and tablet.
  • Standardized ASTM 4 Point System: Ensure global compliance with built-in support for the ASTM 4 Point System , providing consistent grading across all production runs.
  • Dispatch updates: Shown once the product has been dispatched
  • Simplified Reporting: Get daily automated email summaries and standardized MS Excel reports to keep your team informed.

Industrial-Grade Hardware for 24/7 Operations

Reliability is key in a mill environment. FIDAS Lite utilizes Industrial Grade Touch HMIs designed to withstand the rigors of the factory floor. Choose the size that fits your inspection table:

  • 7" HMI: Compact industrial display built for reliable 24/7 operation.
  • 10" HMI: Mid-size industrial display designed for comfortable 24/7 monitoring.
  • 15.6" HMI: Large high-visibility industrial display built for seamless 24/7 operation.

Smart Pricing for Growing Businesses

We believe in making quality control affordable. FIDAS Lite features a transparent, subscription-based model which includes :

  • 6 Months Free: Your first six months of software usage are free from the date of implementation.
  • Affordable Monthly Fee: After the trial, Transitions to a fixed monthly fee, which is billed on an annual basis.
  • Local Support: Sales and implementation are handled by local partners near you, ensuring you have the support you need, when you need it.

Key Takeaways: Why FIDAS Lite is the Smart Move

  • Versatile Access: Monitor your production from any PC or tablet, web-browser-based interface.
  • Global Quality Standards: Built-in support for the ASTM 4 Point System ensures your fabric meets international grading requirements.
  • Live Intelligence: Make faster decisions with a dispatch update and automatic daily email summaries of your mill's performance.
  • Ready-to-Use Hardware: Choose from compact 7", comfortable 10", or high-visibility 15.6\" industrial touch HMIs specifically built for 24/7 operation.
