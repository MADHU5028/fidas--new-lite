What makes FIDAS Lite different from manual inspection?

FIDAS Lite improves inspection consistency, reduces manual logging, simplifies defect tracking, and enhances textile inspection workflows using industrial monitoring systems.

Is FIDAS Lite suitable for small textile businesses?

Yes. FIDAS Lite is designed for both growing textile units and large-scale manufacturing environments with flexible deployment options.

Can FIDAS Lite support ASTM 4-point grading?

Yes. FIDAS Lite supports standardized ASTM 4-point grading workflows to maintain consistent fabric quality inspection processes.

Does FIDAS Lite integrate with existing inspection machines?

FIDAS Lite is built for industrial environments and can integrate with compatible inspection machine workflows and HMI systems.
